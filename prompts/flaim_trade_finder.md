# Flaim Prompt — Trade Finder

Use this to identify potential trade opportunities.

```text
Analyze my Yahoo fantasy basketball league for trade opportunities.

Use platform="yahoo", sport="basketball", leagueId="466.l.3706", teamId="9", seasonYear=2025.

Use my team, opponent teams, standings, rosters, and category strengths/weaknesses if available.

Identify:
1. Players I should sell high
2. Players I should buy low
3. Teams that match well as trade partners
4. Specific trade offers I could make
5. What I should offer
6. What I should seek in return
7. Why the other team might accept
8. Category impact before and after the trade
9. Risk level for each proposed trade
10. Whether each proposed trade is fair, aggressive, conservative, or unrealistic

Prioritize trades that improve my weak categories without destroying my strengths.
```
