# Flaim Prompt — Weekly Matchup Audit

Use this before making weekly lineup or streaming decisions.

```text
Audit my current weekly matchup in Yahoo fantasy basketball.

Use platform="yahoo", sport="basketball", leagueId="466.l.3706", teamId="9", seasonYear=2025.

Summarize:
1. My opponent, if available
2. Current matchup score by category, if available
3. Categories I am leading comfortably
4. Categories I am losing badly
5. Swing categories that are still winnable
6. Roster injuries or GTD players affecting the matchup
7. Streaming priorities for the rest of the week
8. Whether I should chase counting stats or protect percentages/turnovers

Recommend the top 3 category priorities for the rest of the scoring period.
```
