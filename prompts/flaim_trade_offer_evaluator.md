# Flaim Prompt — Trade Offer Evaluator

Use this when evaluating a specific trade offer.

```text
Evaluate this Yahoo fantasy basketball trade offer for my team.

Use platform="yahoo", sport="basketball", leagueId="466.l.3706", teamId="9", seasonYear=2025.

Trade offer:
I give: [PASTE PLAYERS]
I receive: [PASTE PLAYERS]

Evaluate:
1. Rest-of-season value impact
2. Category gains and losses
3. Fit with my current roster
4. Impact on scarce categories
5. Positional impact
6. Injury/status risk
7. Schedule or playoff risk, if available
8. Whether this is fair, an overpay, a steal, or too risky
9. Whether I should accept, reject, or counter
10. A suggested counteroffer if appropriate

Be blunt. Do not approve a trade just because it is close in total value if it makes my category build worse.
```
